Section 2.1 - Institutional mission

The institution has a clearly defined, comprehensive, and published mission specific to the institution and appropriate for higher education. The mission addresses teaching and learning and, where applicable, research and public service. [CR]

Narrative:

Florida Gulf Coast University (FGCU) has a clearly defined, comprehensive, and published mission statement that is specific to the institution and appropriate for higher education that addresses teaching and learning, public service, and research.

Clearly Defined and Comprehensive

The current mission statement was approved by the FGCU Board of Trustees on December 7, 2023 and the SUS Board of Governors on January 24, 2024.

Florida Gulf Coast University (FGCU) is a transformative, learner-centered institution dedicated to student success that strategically serves Southwest Florida and beyond. FGCU, as a regional comprehensive institution, provides academic, economic, and cultural enrichment for our community through impactful partnerships and excellent teaching, learning, scholarship, and public service. We create pathways for our students to thrive as engaged, responsible, global citizens. Through undergraduate and graduate programs, FGCU students receive a distinctive education with experiential learning, community service, and participation in a living laboratory of sustainability practices.

The mission clearly defines FGCU’s purpose as a “regional comprehensive institution that “serves Southwest Florida.” The statement renders visible our “teaching, learning, scholarship, and public service” focus and names the levels of degrees we offer.

Appropriate for Higher Education

In April 2022, the President’s Cabinet and Florida Gulf Coast University’s (FGCU) Board of Trustees implemented the FGCU Strategic Roadmap Initiative, a framework to facilitate the development of the 2024-2029 Strategic Plan. This began a two year-long self-study to include a review of FGCU accountability and effectiveness measures, as well as extensively engaging internal and external constituents in determining the priorities for our next plan. Our process was informed relevant governing documents:

  • Florida Gulf Coast University Focus 2017-2022 (FGCU’s former Strategic Plan)
  • The State University System Strategic Plan
  • The 2025 State University System Strategic Plan for Online Education
  • Florida Gulf Coast University’s 2023 Accountability Plan

Additionally, all FGCU planning processes are aligned with approved accountability and effectiveness measures. The most recent annual reports presented by the President and members of the President’s Cabinet to the FGCU Board of Trustees served as a foundation for building our next strategic plan:

  • FOCUS 2017-2022 Milestone Report, presented at the Kick-Off Meeting for the new strategic planning process on April 18, 2022
  • FOCUS Accomplishments, Published December 2022
  • FGCU Accountability Plan

FGCU also utilized externally normed benchmark measures of student and faculty engagement as measure of effectiveness to support continuous improvement:

  • The National Survey of Student Engagement (NSSE 2023)
  • The Collaborative on Academic Careers in Higher Education (COACHE 2023)

Finally, FGCU’s strategic planning practices continue to be informed by research reports focusing on the present and future state of higher education and the economy both in Florida and our service region:

  • The Blueprint to Secure Florida’s Future: Key Targets & Strategies (Florida 2030)
  • Southwest Florida Educational and Workforce Outcomes Report (2023)

After President Timur was confirmed by the Florida Board of Governors on June 22, 2023, she and the President’s Cabinet designated a Strategic Planning Steering Committee to analyze the Strategic Roadmap Initiative and begin updating the University’s Mission and Vision statements and set goals and objectives for the 2024-2029 plan. FGCU also engaged an external consultant from CampusWorks to bring national expertise and support for the planning process.

With an aim towards continuous improvement, our strategic planning process involved a broad-based involvement of the FGCU Board of Trustees, the President and the President’s Cabinet, Faculty Senate, the Staff Advisory Council, the Black Faculty and Staff Association, the Alliance for Hispanic/Latino/a/x Employees of FGCU (Alianza), the Student Government Association, FGCU alumni, the Office of Planning and Institutional Performance, Academic Deans, Program Directors, and the entire FGCU Community and regional stakeholders via surveys, workshops, and feedback sessions.

Upon the Strategic Plan Steering Committee completing the first draft of Goals and Objectives, it engaged with stakeholder groups including the FGCU Board of Trustees, the Provost’s Council, Academic Affairs Leadership Team, Faculty Senate, Staff Advisory Council, and the President’s Advisory Council on Workforce Development.

“Innovating for Student Success: FGCU’s Journey to Excellence” is the result of the Strategic Roadmap Initiative, the subsequent work of the Strategic Planning Steering Committee, and the engagement of all internal stakeholders and community partners. We are proud of our mission, vision, and overarching goals and objectives that will guide the planning, implementation of tactics, coordinated efforts, assessment, and continuous improvement of all divisions of the University.

The oversight provided by the Board of Governors assures that the mission statements of institutions that constitute the State University System are appropriate for higher education and meet the needs of the State.

Florida Statutes 1001.706 provides that the “Board of Governors shall periodically review the mission of each constituent university and make updates or revisions as needed. Upon completion of a review of the mission, the board shall review existing academic programs for alignment with the mission”. The recent review reconfirmed the appropriate nature of FGCU’s mission to meet the higher education needs of Florida within the context of the State University System.

Published

Florida Gulf Coast University’s mission statement is closely aligned with the five goals of the FGCU’s 2024-2029 Strategic Plan: Innovating for Student Success – FGCU’s Journey to Excellence and is operationalized through it (see CR 7.1). The mission statement is published on the University website, in the strategic plan, online faculty resource guide , and the online student handbook contains a live link to the University mission statement.
